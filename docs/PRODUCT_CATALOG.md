## SKU Naming Conventions

### Format Structure
`MTBC-{CATEGORY}-{SUBCATEGORY}-{SPECS}-{MATERIAL}-{COLOR}`

### Category Codes
- `HB` - Handlebars
- `ST` - Stems
- `GR` - Grips
- `SD` - Saddles
- `DP` - Dropper Posts
- `PD` - Pedals
- `WS` - Wheelsets
- `RIM` - Rims
- `BR` - Brakes
- `RO` - Rotors
- `CR` - Chainrings

### Material Codes
- `AL` - Aluminum
- `CF` - Carbon Fiber
- `STL` - Steel
- `SYN` - Synthetic
- `LTH` - Leather

### Color Codes
- `BLK` - Black
- `SLV` - Silver / Raw Aluminum
- `BLU` - Blue
- `RED` - Red
- `ORG` - Orange
- `GRN` - Green
- `PUR` - Purple
- `WHT` - White
- `GRY` - Gray
- `BRN` - Brown

## Pricing Structure

### Price Tiers
- **Entry Level**: $18 - $50 (Grips, basic components)
- **Mid-Range**: $50 - $150 (Handlebars, stems, pedals, saddles)
- **Premium**: $150 - $350 (Dropper posts, carbon components)
- **High-End**: $350+ (Carbon wheelsets, premium builds)

### Pricing Factors
- Material (Aluminum vs Carbon)
- Manufacturing complexity
- Weight optimization
- Brand positioning (premium)
- Market competition
